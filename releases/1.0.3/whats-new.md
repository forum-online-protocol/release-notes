# What's New In Forum 1.0.3

**Version:** `1.0.3 (build 6)`  
**Prepared:** `2026-03-10`

This update makes Forum feel much closer to a complete product. You can now publish posts and initiatives, discuss them in threads, like content, manage your profile more comfortably, and use a smoother voting flow with better reliability.

## Highlights

### Publish And Participate

- You can publish posts and initiatives through the app with a cleaner creation flow.
- Comments and threaded discussions are now much better supported.
- Likes and post interactions feel more complete and consistent.
- Public timelines are cleaner because pending content stays out of the main feed until it is approved.

### Better Profiles

- Profile and public profile screens were significantly improved.
- Editing profile information is more stable and easier to understand.
- Settings and account-related flows are more consistent.

### Better Voting Experience

- Initiative voting flow is clearer and more reliable.
- Vote publication and verification flows are more reliable and transparent.

### UI And UX Polish

- Feed, post detail, and profile screens received a major polish pass.
- Posting loaders, counters, and navigation behavior are improved.
- Android back behavior is more predictable.
- Localization and copy consistency were cleaned up across the app.

### Foundations For What Comes Next

- We prepared the groundwork for a full biometric flow in a future version.
- We also prepared the groundwork for more public node / public RPC infrastructure as the network becomes more open.

## Also Improved Behind The Scenes

- Better moderation flow for user-generated content.
- Stronger reliability in signed actions and vote handling.
- More production-ready Sepolia on-chain flow for publishing and voting.

## In Short

Forum is now much more than passport onboarding and wallet setup. This release turns it into a more complete social voting app with publishing, discussion, profile improvements, future biometric groundwork, and a more polished day-to-day experience.
